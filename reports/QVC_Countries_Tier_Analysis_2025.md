## Appendix A: Methodology

### Tier Classification

Professions are classified into tiers based on their share of total VP allocation for each nationality:

- **Tier 1 (Primary):** Share >= 15%
- **Tier 2 (Secondary):** Share >= 5% and < 15%
- **Tier 3 (Minor):** Share >= 1% and < 5%
- **Tier 4 (Unusual):** Share < 1%

### Dominance Alert Levels

- **WATCH:** Single profession share >= 30%
- **HIGH:** Single profession share >= 40%
- **CRITICAL:** Single profession share >= 50% (blocking threshold)

## Appendix B: Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **VP** | Visa Permit - Authorization for employment of foreign workers |
| **QVC** | Qatar Visa Center - Overseas processing centers for worker visas |
| **Total VP** | Number of visa permits allocated to establishments |
| **Used VP** | Number of visa permits currently in use |
| **Unused VP** | Available visa permit capacity (Total - Used) |
| **Utilization** | Percentage of allocated VPs currently in use |
| **Dominance** | Concentration of workers in a single profession |
